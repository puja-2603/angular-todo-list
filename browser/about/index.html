About Todos-List

A simple yet effective way to manage your tasks and boost your productivity.

Welcome to the Todos-List application! This platform is designed to help you stay organized and focused. Add, mark, or delete your tasks with ease and never miss out on important things in your day-to-day life.

Whether you're a student, a professional, or just someone looking to stay on top of their to-dos, this app has got you covered!

Features

  • Simple and intuitive UI for easy task management
  • Mark tasks as done with a single click
  • Delete tasks once completed
  • Built with Angular for a seamless experience
